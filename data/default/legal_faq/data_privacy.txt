Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance Policy

Data Protection Officer (DPO): privacy@company.com — contact for subject access requests and data processing questions.

Personal data retention: Employee HR records are retained for seven (7) years after employment ends unless a longer period is required by law.

Lawful bases: Processing is based on contract performance, legal obligation, or legitimate interest as documented in the Records of Processing Activities (ROPA).

Cross-border transfers: Transfers outside the EEA require Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or an adequacy decision approved by Legal.

Breach notification: Suspected personal data breaches must be reported to the DPO within 24 hours of discovery.

Employee training: Mandatory privacy awareness training is required annually for all staff with access to personal data.
