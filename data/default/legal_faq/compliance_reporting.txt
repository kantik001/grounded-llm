Compliance Reporting and Ethics Policy

Whistleblower hotline: Report suspected violations anonymously at ethics-hotline@company.com or +1-800-555-0199 (24/7).

Non-retaliation: The company prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports concerns in good faith.

Annual compliance training: All employees must complete compliance training by 31 March each year.

Gifts and hospitality: Gifts from vendors over USD 100 aggregate per year require manager and Compliance approval.

Conflicts of interest: Disclose potential conflicts using the COI form within 10 business days of becoming aware.

Investigations: Compliance will acknowledge reports within 48 hours and provide status updates at least every 30 days during active investigations.
