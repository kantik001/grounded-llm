IT Portal and service levels (demo IT knowledge base).

IT Portal URL: https://it.company.internal (internal network or VPN required).

Support hours: Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 local time (excluding public holidays).

Service level targets:
- P1 (production down / security incident): initial response within 1 hour
- P2 (major degradation): initial response within 4 hours
- P3 (general request): initial response within 1 business day

Status updates are posted on the ticket in the IT Portal.
